## Purpose

Take stock of where you are and what you have learned. Identifies patterns, assesses progress against the plan, and recommends adjustments.

## Protocol

1. **Review journal entries** — what decisions, discoveries, and gaps have accumulated?
2. **Assess progress against the outline** — what's done, what remains?
3. **Identify patterns** — recurring challenges, effective strategies, knowledge gaps
4. **Recommend adjustments** — should the plan change based on what you have learned?

## Output

A progress summary with:

- Completed vs remaining work
- Key insights from the journal
- Suggested adjustments to the plan
- Confidence assessment — are you on track?
