# /finalize

Prepare the final governance artifact for filing, publication, or formal adoption.

## Protocol

1. **Check all review issues are addressed** from `04-review/`
2. **Run final constitutional compliance validation** against all relevant clauses
3. **Verify independence preservation** in the final artifact
4. **Confirm transparency requirements are met** (rationale documented, suitable for public disclosure)
5. **Polish the output** - formatting, consistency, completeness
6. **Save the final version** to `05-output/`

## Pre-filing checklist

- [ ] All critical review issues resolved
- [ ] All major review issues resolved or documented as known limitations
- [ ] Constitutional basis stated explicitly
- [ ] Rationale documented for every governance decision
- [ ] Suitable for public disclosure
- [ ] Governance officer has approved the final version
- [ ] Filing or publication requirements met (ACRA, website, GitHub)

## Output

Save to `05-output/` with a clear filename. Include:
- The final governance artifact
- A brief summary of what was produced
- Constitutional basis
- Any known limitations

## Journal Entry

Record what was learned during this governance task in `journal/`:
- What governance precedents were set
- What constitutional interpretations were made
- What process improvements should be considered
